## Contact

The lab is based at the University of Arizona and works across tropical forests, alpine systems, trait courses, and biodiversity informatics collaborations. For research, training, data, or collaboration inquiries, the fastest route is still direct email.

### Best Way To Reach Out

- **Prospective graduate students and postdocs:** send a brief research statement, CV, and links to relevant papers, code, or data projects.
- **Collaborators and visiting researchers:** include the project question, expected timeline, and any funding or data context.
- **Media, teaching, or speaking requests:** mention the intended audience, format, and deadline in the first message.
