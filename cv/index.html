Professional Summary

Professor at the University of Arizona, External Professor at the Santa Fe Institute, Honorary Research Associate at Oxford, and Co-director of Bridging Biodiversity and Conservation Science. Research focuses on macroecology, scaling theory, trait-based ecology, biodiversity informatics, and predictive biodiversity science.
